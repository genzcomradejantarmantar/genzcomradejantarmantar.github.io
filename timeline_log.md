# Jantar Mantar Protest Timeline Log

This document serves as the official registry of all events, incidents, and updates regarding the Jantar Mantar peaceful protest and the subsequent police crackdown.

---

### [July 23, 2026, 05:00 PM] - Peaceful Gathering
- **Event:** Students and citizens gathered peacefully at Jantar Mantar to voice their demands. The assembly was orderly, creative, and strictly non-violent, featuring speeches, protest art, and slogans.
- **Participants:** Students from multiple universities, civil society members, and youth activists.

### [July 23, 2026, 07:30 PM] - Police Mobilization
- **Event:** A large contingent of police forces, barricades, and water cannons were positioned around the protest site. Entry and exit points were restricted, creating a tense atmosphere.

### [July 23, 2026, 08:15 PM] - Sudden Lathi Charge and Violence
- **Event:** Without warning or clear orders to disperse, the police launched a sudden lathi charge on the peaceful protesters.
- **Observations:**
  - Protesters were chased and beaten with batons.
  - Several students, including minors and young children present with their families, sustained severe injuries.
  - Female students reported severe harassment, physical mishandling, and inappropriate behavior by the security personnel.
  - Mobile phone signals and internet connectivity in the immediate area were reportedly disrupted to suppress real-time sharing of information.

### [July 23, 2026, 09:20 PM] - Initial Archive Setup
- **Event:** The digital archive was initiated by Comrade to compile all incoming testimonies, media proof, and resources.
- **Status:** Actively collecting evidence. The government and police have attempted to suppress reports, making this archive a critical source of truth.

## Retrospective Log: July 20 – July 22, 2026 (Compiled via Research Agents)

### [July 20, 2026, 11:30 AM] - The "Chalo Sansad" March Commences
- **Event:** Thousands of NEET aspirants and student organization members began a march from Jantar Mantar toward Parliament House, demanding Education Minister Dharmendra Pradhan's resignation and systemic reforms.
- **Participants:** Led by the Cockroach Janta Party (CJP), SFI, AISA, and student volunteers.
- **Security Action:** Police blocked routes at Raisina Road, Shastri Bhawan, and Parliament Street, enforcing Section 163 BNSS.

### [July 20, 2026, 02:00 PM] - Violent Crackdown & Dispersal
- **Event:** Police launched a sudden lathi-charge and fired tear gas into the crowds near Parliament Street.
- **Details:** 
  - Over 170 people (students and police) were injured. Main protest and medical tents at Jantar Mantar were dismantled.
  - Over 90 students were detained, and mobile internet signals in the area were throttled to prevent live streaming.

### [July 21, 2026, 09:00 AM] - Mass Re-assembly at Jantar Mantar
- **Event:** Outraged by the previous day's police brutality, thousands of students re-assembled at the Jantar Mantar site.
- **Details:** Tents were partially re-erected, and coordination resumed under heavy police surveillance.

### [July 21, 2026, 01:00 PM] - CJP Rejects Closed-Door Dialogues
- **Event:** CJP spokespersons Saurav Das and Ashutosh Ranka met Union Minister JP Nadda but formally rejected any private negotiations.
- **Details:** The student leadership declared that talks must happen in a public forum or neutral venue.

### [July 22, 2026, 12:00 PM] - Parliamentary Standstill (Monsoon Session Day 3)
- **Event:** The Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha faced total gridlock as opposition parties demanded a debate under Rule 267 and the minister's resignation.
- **Details:** Parliamentary Affairs Minister Kiren Rijiju stated the government is open to debate but accused the opposition of stalling.

### [July 22, 2026, 04:00 PM] - Sonam Wangchuk Shifted to Medanta Hospital
- **Event:** Under Delhi High Court directives, climate activist Sonam Wangchuk, on hunger strike since June 28 at Jantar Mantar in solidarity, was shifted to Medanta Hospital.
- **Details:** Ministers JP Nadda and Jitendra Singh visited him to convey the government's willingness for dialogue.

### [July 22, 2026, 06:00 PM] - MHA Enforces 1.5-km Internet Suspension
- **Event:** The Ministry of Home Affairs ordered temporary suspension of mobile internet within 1.5-km of Jantar Mantar, barricading all adjacent entry routes.

### [July 23, 2026, 10:00 AM] - PM Modi Announces Fast-Track Courts
- **Event:** Prime Minister Modi broke public silence, announcing dedicated fast-track courts to try paper leak malpractices.
- **Details:** Protesters labeled the announcement a diversion from their demands for the Education Minister's resignation.

### [July 23, 2026, 06:30 PM] - Commercial Lockdowns & RAF Deployment
- **Event:** The New Delhi Traders Association advised Connaught Place shops to close early due to high security cordons and RAF deployment.

### [July 23, 2026, 09:30 PM] - Nationwide Protest Call for July 24
- **Event:** CJP and allied student unions issued a nationwide call for protest on Friday, July 24, 2026, titled *"Every District. One Day. One Demand."*
- **Status:** Mobilization in progress across college campuses and districts nationwide in response to the evening lathi-charge.
